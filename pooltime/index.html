How to use Pool Time

  • Start the clock — the countdown begins automatically; tap the screen to reset it for the next shot.
  • Extensions — give the Red or Yellow player extra time with the extension buttons.
  • Pause / Resume / Reset — full control whenever play stops.
  • Settings — adjust the timer duration, extension length, warning thresholds, sounds, vibration, theme, and warning colour.
  • Achievements — tap the trophy to view your progress, charts, and activity heatmap.

Privacy Policy

Pool Time does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app has no accounts and no sign-up.

All settings and achievement data are stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to us or to any third party. If you uninstall the app, that data is removed with it.
