Pool Time — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

The short version: Pool Time does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-up, no ads, and no analytics or tracking. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

None. Pool Time does not collect any personal information about you. The app has no user accounts, no login, and no registration of any kind.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Your preferences and progress are saved only on your device using local storage, so the app remembers your setup between sessions. This includes:

  • Settings such as timer duration, extension time, warning thresholds, sounds, vibration, theme, and warning colour.
  • Your achievements and progress history.

This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or anyone else, and is removed if you uninstall the app. If you use the in-app backup feature, any exported file is shared by you, to a destination you choose, and is not sent to us.

3. No Tracking, Analytics, or Advertising

Pool Time contains no advertising, no analytics or tracking SDKs, and no crash-reporting tools. We do not build any profile of you and do not use cookies or device advertising identifiers.

4. Device Permissions

The app may use your device's vibration/haptics to provide timer alerts. This is used purely to give feedback during play and does not involve collecting any data. Pool Time does not access your location, contacts, photos, camera, or microphone.

5. Third-Party Services

Pool Time has no backend servers and integrates no third-party data services. The app is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play; those platforms may collect certain information (for example, download and basic diagnostic data) under their own privacy policies, which are outside our control.

6. Children's Privacy

Pool Time is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised version at this address. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Get in touch at kloeden@gmail.com.
